Timeline

2016.04.22

James Spring launches

The studio's first game. A hide-and-seek runner where one tap makes you dash and jump, hunting for 44 hidden friends. This is where it all began.

2016.09.03

James Summer

An endless waterslide summer arcade. The second game, released the same year.

2018.07.04

Cats are Cute launches

A healing idle game about collecting cats and growing a tiny town — still running today, the studio's flagship. App Store Featured · Google Play Editors' Choice, ★4.9 with 180K+ reviews.

2021.05.12

Cats are Cute: Pop Time!

A Suika-style cat merge puzzle. Indie Games Festival 2021 Top 20, App Store Game of the Day, and even a cameo in a Japanese TV drama.

2023.12.01

10 million downloads

Crossed by 'Cats are Cute' alone — and still counting, every day.

2026

The tenth year

What started as a game studio has grown into a wider lineup of apps and games — Than Yesterday, PinClip, Talk Memo, Honest Camera, Palette 2048... and it keeps going.
