DETAILS06

Small app. Parent-grade decisions.

Saves to your Photos library

Photos don't get trapped inside the app — they land in the family library at full quality, the moment they're taken.

Paid once. That's it.

No subscription, no coins, no “premium”. Inside the app there is nothing to buy and no ad to tap.

Lock them in, gently

Onboarding walks you through Apple's Guided Access — triple-click the side button and your child can't leave the camera.

Volume-button shutter? Removed on purpose.

Toddlers can't tell the volume button from the power button — and the screen goes dark. So the only shutter is on the screen.

What they see is what you get

No filters, no AI touch-ups, no stickers. The photo is exactly what your child saw. That's why it's called Honest.
