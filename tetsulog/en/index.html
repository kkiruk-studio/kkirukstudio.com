帳· HOW IT WORKS01–03

From a tap on the map to a stamp in the book.

LOG01

Tap a station to log it

Tap any station on the map. Tetsulog records the date and the segment you rode.

COLOR02

The map fills in color

Ridden lines spread across the map in color. Unridden stretches stay gray, so your progress is obvious at a glance.

STAMP03

Stamps fill the book

Complete a line and a vermilion “line complete” stamp is pressed. Page by page, the book fills up.
