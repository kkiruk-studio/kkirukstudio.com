Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 27, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to operate and improve our services.

① Automatically collected data (via SDKs and platforms)

  • Advertising identifiers (ADID, IDFA)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, country, app version, etc.)
  • Log data (app launch times, crash reports, error logs, usage statistics, etc.)

② Data collected through platform services (iCloud / Google Play Games)

  • Cloud save data manually created by the user
  • Platform-linked identifiers (Apple ID or Google Account; managed by the platform, not directly collected by us)

③ Optional data (if applicable)

  • Nicknames, gameplay settings, purchase history, etc.

2. Purpose of Collection and Use

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving gameplay and app stability
  • Delivering and measuring advertisements
  • Statistical and analytical research for product quality improvement
  • Legal compliance and dispute resolution

3. Retention and Deletion of Data

  • Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above.
  • Data may be retained for a longer period if required by relevant laws and regulations.

We do not share personal information with external parties except as listed below, and only for legitimate operational reasons.

6. User Rights

You may request to view, correct, or delete your personal information at any time.

  • Requests can be made via email at kkirukstudio.help@gmail.com.

7. Data Destruction

  • Once the purpose of data collection has been achieved, your information will be deleted without delay.
  • Digital data will be permanently deleted using secure technical methods to prevent recovery.

8. Cloud Save Data

  • Our games do not operate independent servers.

Game progress can be backed up or restored via Apple iCloud or Google Play Games.

  • Cloud saves are not automatic — the user must manually perform or confirm the save action within the game or platform interface.
  • Cloud save data is managed by each platform provider. We do not have direct access to or store any user cloud data.

9. Children’s Privacy

  • Our games are not directed toward children under the age of 14 (or 13 where applicable by local law).
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
  • If any child data is found to have been collected, it will be permanently deleted without delay.
